Services

We offer a comfortable environment, loving licensed dog groomers with several years of professional dog grooming experience. You can trust that I will provide your pet with a friendly, professional and caring environment.we specialize in "Gentle Touch" dog grooming. Remember, keeping your pet happy during their stay with us, is always our top priority! From baths and fancy haircuts, to flea and tick treatments, to skin care, moisturizing, anti-dandruff and de-drying treatments, your pampered pet can go home looking and feel wonderful. Our rates are very fair, but of outstanding professional standard. The basic bath kit begins at $20 for small, smooth-coated dogs (bath, nails, anal glands, ear cleaning, cologne and bandana). Complete dog grooming, involves haircut, wash, nails, ear hair removal, glands expressed during bathing. Prices start at $35 for small breeds and rise depending on the size and condition of the coat. All dog grooming services are available by appointment.
